Krabby Patty Secret Recipe!

Description

Want to learn how to make the tastiest, most mouth-watering patty in the entire sea? You have to promise not to tell Plankton though, or his beautiful wife Karen. This delicious recipe will serve 4 landlubbers and should take around 20 minutes. Let's get to it!

Ingredients

    Burger Patties

  • 1 1/3 lb. ground beef (80 percent lean)
  • 2 tsp. Old Bay seasoning
  • 1 tsp. Black pepper
  • 1 tsp. Sea salt
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese

King Neptune's Poseidon Powder Aioli

  • 1/2 c. mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp. Old Bay seasoning

Sandwich

  • 4 sesame seed buns, sliced in half
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 1 jar of dill pickle slices
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 4 leaves of butter lettuce

Directions

    Step 1: Make the Burger Patties

  1. Heat a grill pan or saut√© pan over medium heat. As it heats, combine ground beef, Old Bay, pepper and salt.
  2. Form into four equally sized patties, about 1/3 lb apiece.
  3. Set on a platter next to the stovetop.
  4. Coat the warmed grill pan with cooking spray and place patties on the pan, cooking each side about 4-5 minutes, or until cooked through to your desired doneness.
